About Us

The Peoples Project is a non-profit organization that is dedicated to improving the lives of older people. We provide a range of services that help older people to live independently and with dignity. Our services include home care, social activities, and support for carers. We also campaign for better services for older people and work to raise awareness of the issues that affect them.
